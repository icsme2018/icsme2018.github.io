Venue

Conference Venue

    ICSME 2018 will be held at the beautiful Círculo de Bellas Artes de Madrid (Calle de Alcalá, 42), a cultural center located in the heart of the city's historic center where young Picasso attended as a pupil to his painting classes. The activities of the main conference will take place in the fourth floor, in particular:

  • Plenary sessions will take place in the fantastic Sala de Columnas.
  • Parallel sessions will be in the Sala Antonio Palacios.
  • Poster sessions will take place in the Fuentecilla.

Food

    Spain is known for its good taste and variety of food. ICSME 2018 could not be different and we have organized some special arrangements so that participants enjoy not only the program, but good food in a nice surrounding and (hopefully) the good weather. In particular:

  • Coffee breaks will be in the beautiful terrace of Fábula, just opposite to the Círculo de Bellas Artes. The terrace has a magnificent, protected garden where conference participants can enjoy coffee and pastries while socializing.
  • Lunch will be served in the Las Bóvedas de Cibeles Restaurant, crossing Alcaláa street. Instead of a standing cocktail, we have chosen a typical Spanish restaurant with a special taste, where the menú del día can be appreciated, which includes two courses and dessert. Conference participants may accompany their meal with local wine and beer, if desired (and at no cost).

From the airport

    The local airport, Madrid-Barajas, is easily reachable by air, with direct connections to major European and other hubs. From there to the venue, you have several options:

  • Express bus: There is an express bus departing every 15-20 minutes that takes you for 5€ from the airport to Plaza de Cibeles in around 35 minutes. Plaza de Cibeles is 300 meters away from the venue.
  • Metro: The metro costs 4.5 euro (single ticket) and departs every 5-10 minutes. Travel time is around 45 minutes and you will have to change twice to get to the venue. The closest stops are Banco de España (close to the Plaza de Cibeles) and Sevilla (close to Plaza de Sol), both on line 2 (red). Having a Metro Map (PDF) of the whole Madrid Metro network is usually very convenient (they can also be obtained on paper in almost any metro station)
  • Taxi: This is the more expensive option; cost is 30 euro (fixed price, including luggage). It takes 30 minutes from the airport (depending on rush hour); credit cards are accepted in all taxis.

Football (Soccer)

    Madrid is world-known for two of its professional football teams, Real Madrid (2018 UEFA Champions Leage winner) and Atlético de Madrid (2018 UEFA Europa League winner).

  • At this point, we do not have information of matches (in the Spanish LaLiga or in the UEFA Champions League) during the conference period, but as soon as we have it (this will be sorrily not before late August) we will post information of how to get tickets.
  • Football fans won't want to miss a visit to the magnificent Santiago Bernabéu, home to the legendary Real Madrid football team. If so, you should do the Bernabéu Tour which takes around 150 minutes and costs 25 €.
  • Apart from visiting the Santiago Bernabéu Stadium, attendees could also visit the new Wanda Metropolitano Stadium and see its facilities, inaugurated in September 2017. The Wanda Metropolitano will host the final game of the UEFA European Champions League in 2019.
