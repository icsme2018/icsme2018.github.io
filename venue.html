Venue

Conference Venue

    ICSME 2018 will be held at the beautiful Círculo de Bellas Artes de Madrid, a cultural center located in the heart of the city's historic center and within walking distance of the major touristic attractions of the city, including its world-renown museums. For example, the distances to the most iconic Madrilenian attractions are:

  • Plaza de Cibeles: 300 meters
  • Thyssen-Bornemisza Museum: 400 meters
  • Puerta del Sol: 650 meters
  • Retiro Park (Puerta de Alcalá): 700 meters
  • Prado Museum: 750 meters
  • Gran Vía (Callao): 800 meters
  • Plaza Mayor: 1.1 km
  • Reina Sofía Museum: 1.3 km
  • Royal Palace: 1.6 km
  • Bernabéu stadion: 4.3 km (there are several buses from Plaza de Cibeles that take you there in 15 minutes)

Where to stay

    There are plenty of Hotels in Madrid within walking distance of the conference venue, from budget to exclusive.

    The closest hotel, 20 meters from the venue away, is Hotel NH Collection Madrid Suecia (*****). Renovated in 2016, the hotel was originally opened in 1956 and served as Ernest Hemingway's and the revolutionary Che Guevara's Madrid home.

From the airport

    The local airport, Madrid-Barajas, is easily reachable by air, with direct connections to major European and other hubs. From there to the venue, you have several options:

  • Express bus: There is an express bus that takes you for 5€ from the airport to Plaza de Cibeles in around 35 minutes. Plaza de Cibeles is 300 meters away from the venue.
  • Metro: The metro costs 4.5 euro (single ticket) and departs every 5-10 minutes. Travel time is around 45 minutes and you will have to change twice to get to the venue. The closest stops are Banco de España (close to the Plaza de Cibeles) and Sevilla (close to Plaza de Sol), both on line 2 (red).
  • Taxi: This is the more expensive option; cost is 30 euro (fixed price, including luggage). It takes 30 minutes from the airport (depending on rush hour); credit cards are accepted in all taxis.

Moving around

    The Metro de Madrid is fast, safe and tidy. Banco de España (at the Plaza de Cibeles), the nearest stop, is served by metro line 2 (red). If you do not stay close to the conference venue, it may be interesting to buy a 10-ride ticket. See Season tickets and discounted fares.
