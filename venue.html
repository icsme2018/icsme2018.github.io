Conference Venue

    ICSME 2018 will be held at the beautiful Círculo de Bellas Artes de Madrid (Calle de Alcalá, 42), a cultural center located in the heart of the city's historic center where young Picasso attended as a pupil to his painting classes. The activities of the main conference will take place in the fourth floor, in particular:

  • Plenary sessions will take place in the Sala de Columnas

Public transport

    The Metro de Madrid is fast, safe and tidy. Banco de España (at the Plaza de Cibeles) and Sevilla, the nearest stops, are served by metro line 2 (red). Having a Metro Map (PDF) of the whole Madrid Metro network is usually very convenient (they can also be obtained on paper in almost any metro station). From the airport

    The local airport, Madrid-Barajas, is easily reachable by air, with direct connections to major European and other hubs. From there to the venue, you have several options:

  • Express bus: There is an express bus departing every 15-20 minutes that takes you for 5€ from the airport to Plaza de Cibeles in around 35 minutes. Plaza de Cibeles is 300 meters away from the venue.
  • Metro: The metro costs 4.5 euro (single ticket) and departs every 5-10 minutes. Travel time is around 45 minutes and you will have to change twice to get to the venue. The closest stops are Banco de España (close to the Plaza de Cibeles) and Sevilla (close to Plaza de Sol), both on line 2 (red). Having a Metro Map (PDF) of the whole Madrid Metro network is usually very convenient (they can also be obtained on paper in almost any metro station)
  • Taxi: This is the more expensive option; cost is 30 euro (fixed price, including luggage). It takes 30 minutes from the airport (depending on rush hour); credit cards are accepted in all taxis.

Where to stay

    There are plenty of Hotels in Madrid within walking distance of the conference venue, from budget to exclusive.

    The closest hotel, a couple of meters away from the venue, is the exclusive (and rather expensive) Hotel NH Collection Madrid Suecia (*****). Renovated in 2016, the hotel was originally opened in 1956 and served as Ernest Hemingway's and the revolutionary Che Guevara's Madrid home.

    More economic options, but still very close are:

  • Hotel Lusso Infantas (****, 150m)
  • Hotel Petit Palace Alcalá (****, 200m)
  • Hotel Petit Palace Lealtad Plaza (****, 200m)
  • Hotel One Shot Prado 23 (****, 200m)
  • Hotel One Shot Recoletos 04 (****, 250m)
  • Hotel Quatro Puerta del Sol (****, 250m)
  • Hotel Casual Madrid del Teatro (**, 250m)
  • Hotel Petit Palace Chueca (***, 300m)

    For the budget attendant, closeby you can find the Hostal Las Cortes, Hostal Oporto, Hostal Prim, Hostal Delfina and, a little further away, Hostal Gallardo. All these hostels are tidy and clean, have wifi, air conditioning and each room has its own bathroom.

Nearby attractions

    Within walking distance of the Círculo de Bellas Artes you will be able to find the major touristic attractions of the city, including its world-renown museums. For example, the distances to the most iconic Madrilenian attractions are:

  • Plaza de Cibeles: 300 meters
  • Thyssen-Bornemisza Museum: 400 meters
  • Puerta del Sol: 650 meters
  • Retiro Park (Puerta de Alcalá): 700 meters
  • Prado Museum: 750 meters
  • Gran Vía (Callao): 800 meters
  • Plaza Mayor: 1.1 km
  • Reina Sofía Museum: 1.3 km
  • Royal Palace: 1.6 km
  • Bernabéu stadion: 4.3 km (there are several buses, number 14, 27 or 150, stopping at Plaza de Cibeles that take you there in less than 15 minutes)

The Community of Madrid offers a Map of the Central Zone of Madrid (PDF), with dimensions of 48 x 33 cm, especially suitable for tourists visiting our city. The map can be obtained as well in the various Tourist Information Points of the Community, such as the one at the Madrid Barajas airport.
