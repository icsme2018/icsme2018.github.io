ICSME Pre-reception

Tuesday, September 25th from 17:30 to 21:30 at Plaza de Manuel Becerra

    All ICSME, SCAM, VISSOFT and DysDoc participants are invited (at not cost) to the pre-reception on Tuesday evening. The pre-reception will start at 17:30 and finish around 21:30, and food and drinks will be served in an informal atmosphere where there will be tool demos and poster presentations.

    Authors of doctoral symposium, NIER, tool demo and short industry papers can present a poster on Tuesday evening in the pre-reception, in addition to their presentation at the main conference. Registration for ICSME will be open at the pre-reception from 18:00 to 21:00.

    The pre-reception won't be in the main venue, but in a building of the Fundación Universidad Rey Juan Carlos in the city center of Madrid (see picture below), especifically in the Plaza Manuel Becerra, 14.

    The Metro de Madrid is fast, safe and tidy, and the best way to get there. The venue is close to the Plaza Manuel Becerra metro station, served by two metro lines: line 2 (red) and line 6 (gray, the circular one). From the main venue (the Círculo de Bellas Artes), the Plaza de Manuel Becerra metro station can be reached in 12 minutes using line 2 (red).
