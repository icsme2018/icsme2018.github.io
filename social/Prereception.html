ICSME Pre-reception

Tuesday, September 25th from 17:30 to 21:00

    The pre-reception won't be in the main venue, but in a building of the Fundación Universidad Rey Juan Carlos in the city center of Madrid, especifically in the Plaza Manuel Becerra. The Metro de Madrid is fast, safe and tidy, and the best way to get there. The Plaza Manuel Becerra metro station is served by two metro lines: line 2 (red) and line 6 (gray, the circular one). From the main venue, the Plaza de Manuel Becerra metro station can be reached in 12 minutes using line 2 (red).

    All participants to ICSME, SCAM, VISSOFT and DySDoc3 are invited (at no cost) to join the pre-reception.
